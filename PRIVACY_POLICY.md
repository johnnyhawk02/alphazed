**Last Updated:** March 29, 2025

## Introduction

AlphaZed ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and all users of our educational application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information and your child's information when you use our mobile application AlphaZed (the "Application").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Application.

## Information Collection

### Information We DO NOT Collect

AlphaZed is designed with children's privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from users, including:

- Personal identifiers (names, email addresses, physical addresses, phone numbers, etc.)
- Geolocation information
- Photos, videos, or audio files containing children's images or voices
- Screen or device recordings
- Persistent identifiers that can be used to recognize a user over time and across different websites or online services
- Usage data or analytics that could identify individual users

### Device Information

The Application does not collect any device information beyond what is minimally necessary for the functioning of the app, which includes:

- Device type and operating system version
- App performance data

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

## How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your information for any purposes beyond ensuring the proper functioning of the Application on your device.

## Data Storage and Security

AlphaZed stores all game progress and settings locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

AlphaZed does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services that would collect or process user data.

## Children's Privacy

AlphaZed is designed for use by children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws. As stated above, we do not collect personal information from any users, including children under the age of 13.
